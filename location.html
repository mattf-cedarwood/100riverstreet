Why Springfield

A Community on the Rise

Springfield was once a thriving manufacturing center anchored by the precision tool and die industry. After decades of transition, the town is actively working toward revitalization.

  • The town has invested in downtown infrastructure improvements.
  • Springfield Regional Development Corporation is actively recruiting businesses.
  • Springfield on the Move serves as the de facto chamber of commerce, driving economic development.
  • Black River Innovation Campus (BRIC) has become a hub for entrepreneurship.
  • Central location in Windsor County with easy access via I-91 and Route 11.
