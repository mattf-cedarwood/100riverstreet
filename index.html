The Story

A Century of Making, Reimagined

Originally built by the Fellows Gear Shaper Company — a cornerstone employer in Springfield for over 100 years — this 255,000 SF campus is being transformed into a thriving mixed-use destination on the banks of the Black River.

Springfield Medical Center and an on-site pharmacy already call 100 River Street home. The next chapter includes a full-service restaurant, brewery, manufacturing spaces, retail storefronts, and riverfront community gathering areas.

  • 255,000 square feet of versatile space
  • Riverfront location on the Black River
  • Central Business District zoning
  • Anchor tenants already in place
  • On-site parking with 30,000+ SF garage
